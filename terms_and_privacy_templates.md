```markdown
# Privacy Policy
**Last Updated: July 16, 2026**

LedgerIQ Suite ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your business and personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, process, and protect your information in compliance with the **Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173)**.

---

## 1. Information We Collect
We collect information necessary to run your business operations:
*   **Account Details:** Business name, owner email address, corporate tax identification numbers (TIN), and contact information.
*   **Employee Information:** Names, attendance records, biometric/PIN hashes, and payroll details (if utilizing the HR module).
*   **Transactional Data:** Sales logs, client databases, stock levels, vendor unit costs, and general ledger journal postings.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information strictly to:
*   Authenticate logins and active device sessions.
*   Provide real-time inventory deductions, POS checkouts, and financial reporting.
*   Process secure token authentication and prevent concurrent login breaches.
*   Resolve technical support requests.
*   *We will never sell or share your business transaction history, client lists, or financial ledgers with third-party advertisers.*

## 3. Data Storage & Security
*   **Location:** Your data is stored securely in encrypted cloud servers. All passwords, session tokens, and PIN keys are hashed before database insertion.
*   **Session Logs:** The system monitors active sessions to prevent concurrent logins on unauthorized devices. Session logs are automatically cleared once a session is closed or expires due to inactivity.

## 4. Your Rights (under the DPA)
You and your registered employees retain the right to:
*   Request access to the personal data we store.
*   Request corrections to any inaccurate records.
*   Request complete deletion of your workspace data upon account cancellation.

## 5. Contact Information
For data privacy inquiries or requests, please contact our Data Protection Officer at:
*   **Email:** privacy@ledgeriqsuite.com
*   **Developer:** CC Studios
```
